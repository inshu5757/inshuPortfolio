Understanding Dosages

  • Children vs. Adult Dosage: Dosage differs based on age and body weight.
  • Follow Prescriptions: Always follow the doctor's prescription for safe usage.
  • Reading Labels: Important to check dosage, side effects, and expiration dates.

Storage and Disposal

  • Store in a cool, dry place, away from sunlight.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Dispose of expired or unused medicines safely.
